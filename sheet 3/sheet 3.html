Types of personal computers

Personal computers , also known as PCs, are the least powerful, yet the most widely used and fastest-growing type of computer. There are five types of personal computers: desktops, laptops, tablets, smartphones, and wearables.

Desktop computers are small enough to fit on top of or alongside a desk yet are too big to carry around.

Laptop computers , also known as notebook computers, are portable and lightweight and fit into most briefcases.

Tablets also known as tablet computers, are smaller, lighter, and generally less powerful than laptops. Like a laptop, tablets have a flat screen but typically do not have a standard keyboard. Instead, tablets typically use a virtual keyboard that appears on the screen and is touch-sensitive.
